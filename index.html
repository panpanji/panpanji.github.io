About Me

I am an environmental scientist with an MSc in Environmental Monitoring, Modelling and Reconstruction from the University of Manchester. My research focuses on understanding and predicting ecosystem responses to environmental change through remote sensing, spatial modelling, and trustworthy geospatial AI. I am passionate about data-driven approaches for ecosystem restoration and environmental decision support, with field experience in peatland, alpine, and semi-arid systems.

Research Experience

MSc Dissertation: Modelling ecosystem functional recovery at Bolton Fell Moss
Feb 2025 – Aug 2025 · with Dr Emma Shuttleworth, University of Manchester
Developed a multi-source spatial framework to quantify and attribute spatiotemporal changes in ecosystem functions following peatland restoration under data-limited conditions. Harmonised LULC, DEM, and climatic datasets, and produced restoration-specific land-cover classes. Quantified area-weighted functional shifts (2015–2020), revealing a 79.5% reduction in nitrogen export and 82.4% reduction in sediment export as indicators of recovering eco-hydrological processes. Mapped spatial redistribution of high-risk zones and critically evaluated model applicability under data-limited conditions.
Drought microcosm experiments: plant physiological responses
Feb 2025 – Jun 2025 · with Dr Filipa Cox and Dr Emily Brooks, University of Manchester
Investigated how ectomycorrhizal fungi regulate plant drought resistance. Measured plant physiological responses using leaf gas exchange (LI-6800) and chlorophyll fluorescence (FluorPen), alongside soil physicochemical characterisation (pH, C/N). Processed plant and soil samples for biomass and elemental analyses, ensuring experimental consistency across treatments linking drought intensity, microbial mediation, and plant stress responses.
Sustainability assessment of conservation tillage systems
with Prof. Zhixin Zhang, Northwest A&F University
Evaluated productivity and ecological sustainability of rapeseed-maize rotation systems under conservation tillage. Conducted Life Cycle Assessment (LCA) to quantify global warming potential, energy use, and water footprint. Supported construction of an integrated evaluation framework and manuscript preparation.
BSc Dissertation: Effects of Organic Fertiliser Amendment in Alpine Mining Areas
with Prof. Jiankun Kou, Northwest A&F University
Investigated effects of organic fertiliser on plant growth and soil properties in degraded alpine sites through field sampling, lab analysis, and statistical comparisons.
Germination trait variation under temperature and drought stress
Jan 2022 – Jan 2023 · with Prof. Zhixin Zhang
Examined germination responses of 14 native species from the Qinghai-Tibet Plateau under temperature (10°C–20°C) and water potential gradients (PEG-6000 simulated drought). Quantified germination percentage, mean germination time, and rate index. Contributed to a peer-reviewed publication.
Water-root interaction of forage corn/Alfalfa regulated by biochar
Nov 2021 – Feb 2022 · with Prof. Zhixin Zhang
Participated in a two-year field experiment in the semi-arid Loess Plateau, investigating biochar effects on plant water use and interspecific competition in maize-alfalfa intercropping. Assisted with field sampling, root trait analysis, and soil-plant data processing.
Coursework-based modelling projects (MSc level)
